Your generosity goes so far

Your gift will provide emergency shelter for children, adults, and families, and ensure 24/7 SAFEline support is available. Together, we can provide crucial support services, advocate for justice, and create safe spaces where survivors can rebuild their lives with dignity and strength. It will help fund counseling, legal services, resources for parents, wraparound support for kids, and much more.

Every contribution makes a difference. Donate today and be a beacon of hope for those in need.
