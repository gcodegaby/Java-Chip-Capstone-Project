Contact Us

Reach out to our team !


Our mission at SAFE is to provide support, advocacy, and assistance to survivors of abuse. Whether you need someone to talk to, guidance on your next steps, or resources for healing, we are here for you. Please don't hesitate to fill out the adjoining form for a prompt response from our dedicated team. Additionally, we offer 24-hour assistance; you can call us anytime at our 24-hour SAFEline (512)267-SAFE[7233]. Your well-being is our priority, and we're committed to standing by you every step of the way.
